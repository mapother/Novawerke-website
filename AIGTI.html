AIGTI Mission-Readiness Program

REMOVING BARRIERS TO AMERICAN LEADERSHIP IN ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

The problem

The Impediment to Innovation

Agencies use different formats, vocabularies, and dashboards. Checklists exist, but without a common gauge, results aren’t comparable. This increases cost, creates risk, and slows innovation.

The opportunity

Turn standardization into capability

Enable every federal component to adopt a consistent, interoperable AI governance framework. Using the AIGTI templates, Novawerke helps agencies document compliance, reduce oversight risk, and accelerate safe deployment—creating a unified standard for mission readiness.

Our role

AIGTI: the rail gauge

Novawerke supplies the AI Governance Template Initiative (AIGTI): inventory schema, risk register, model card, waiver flow, and acquisition clause pack—ready for direct agency implementation.

How it works

Implementation phases

PHASE 1

Diagnostic Sprint · 6 weeks

  • Baseline an agency (or simulated environment) using the AIGTI inventory + risk schema.
  • Deliver a 30-60-90 day plan and executive scorecard.
PHASE 2

Framework Build · 12–16 weeks

  • Standing governance charter, risk gates, and waiver authority.
  • Acquisition clause pack + vendor questionnaire.
PHASE 3

Deployment & Validation · 4 weeks

  • Deliver a live Mission-Readiness report and remediation plan.
  • Institutionalize AIGTI artifacts and handoff for sustained use.
